Terita Project

These pages are dedicated to the many ideas I have for games I would like to develop. All these games are located in the same universe, and thus I refer to them all as the Terita Project. For now I'll be filling these pages with random ideas and thoughts that I have for these games, and using this site as a base to develop the world of Terita.

The majority of my ideas will go under General Ideas, as that is dedicated for all the loose ideas I have. Ideas under General Ideas are considered non-canonical for the overall lore of Terita, with the actual canonical events taking place within the games affiliated with Terita.
