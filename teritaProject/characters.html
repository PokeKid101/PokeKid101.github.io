Terita | Characters

This page is dedicated to the various characters within the world of Terita. These include character ideas, concepts, and even notable enemies within the area. This page includes a loosely organized list of people and their general description or purpose.

Teritan Islands

Grandpa

Old gentleman who has been researching the world as much as he can. He's joining this journey across the sea to see if there is anything else beyond the Cornucopia, also he's watching over his grandchild (you). Once you've reached the Teritan Islands, he asks you if you can assist him in researching the creatures on the island known as Terita.

Goober

A common Terita on the Teritan Islands. He's a little slime guy that is very adaptive.
